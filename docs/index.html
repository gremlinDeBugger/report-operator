The pipeline

Four gates between a ticker and your inbox.

01 — FETCH

Pull the quarters

Your key pulls eight quarters of income-statement data from Financial Modeling Prep or Alpha Vantage — your account, your data.

02 — VERIFY

Check every figure

A verification gate recomputes each number in the write-up against the provider payload. Anything it can't confirm is discarded, not printed.

03 — RENDER

Fix the format

The report renders to one fixed template — same layout, same order, every quarter. Comparable across companies and across time.

04 — DELIVER

Email the PDF

The finished report lands in your inbox as an attachment, branded with your firm's name. Or download it straight from this page.

Why a gate? AI-written summaries invent plausible numbers. This pipeline treats that as a defect, not a style choice: the write-up may only state figures the verifier can trace to the source data. Duller sentences, correct numbers.

Bring your own key. Reports pull from your account at Financial Modeling Prep or Alpha Vantage — both offer free keys that cover this report.

How your key is handled: it travels once over HTTPS, is used for this single fetch, and goes out of scope when the run ends. It is never logged, never written to disk, never stored. There is no account system here — nothing to breach. Read the source.
